HAORAN WANG

Ph.D. Candidate · Semiconductor/MEMS Devices ·  wanghaoran@ufl.edu
Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering ·  University of Florida

I am a third-year Ph.D. student supervised by Dr. Huikai Xie. I have been a member of Interdisciplinary Microsystems Group and Biophotonics & Microsystems Lab since 2016, when I came to UF as an exchange student to complete my undergraduate thesis project. I received my Bachelor degree in Measuring and Control Technology and Instrumentations from Tianjin University in 2017.

My research interests include semiconductor/MEMS devices, microfabrication and piezoelectric sensors & actuators, especially focusing on piezoelectric micromachined ultrasonic transducers (pMUTs). Currently, I am working on pMUT arrays for endoscopic photoacoustic imaging applications and piezoelectric MEMS loud speakers. Please refer to my CV for more details.
